A Little About Us

China Restaurant was established in 1994, after Wen-Ching Ho (The owner of the restaurant) closed down a gadget shop that he used to run. China Restaurant was the only Asian restaurant in Kimberley until 2014 when more Asians moved to Kimberley and opened up more restaurants.
